How can you make your first $1,000?

It's not that easy but yeah it's quite possible. As a matter of fact, People are making huge with blogging. Do you have ever heard of Event Blogging? If no then let me tell you, what exactly does it means.

“A blog which is made to target a single day or week or month for receiving the huge amount of traffic is called event niche blog. Example: Happy New Year (Target: one day or week) Indian Premier League (Target: Almost 50 Days) In the short span of time, you can make huge income.” Still with me?

Now, How can you do Event Blogging?
Suppose, today is Valentine day and you have an internet connection and Smartphone or laptop, what will you do?

Most probably, you will search something about valentine day like Happy Valentine Day Wishes Valentine Day Gifts for girlfriend You are not alone in the world; there are millions and billions of people who do similar search about a particular topic.

And mostly they go through Google or any other search engines.

So in event blogging, we do create a blog on a particular topic (eg. Valentine) and by doing search engine optimization we do rank our website at the top position in the search engines.

If my website is on the first page, with a good keyword then I will get millions of traffic in a single day. By monetizing my blog with Advertisements (Adsense), I can make $$$$ in a single day.

Now how to do the same as they do,

=> Choose An Event: Choose an Event which is popular such as a Festival or a Sports (IPL)

=> Buy a Domain: Buy a Keyword domain for example if you are going to target Valentine's Day then Valentine's day should be in the domain name. Such as Valentinesdaywishes.com

=> Keyword Research: Do some keyword research, Check and analyse competition and then work. Keyword research is one of the most important thing for a Event blog.

=> Content Creation: Create content with targetted keywords.

=> Backlinks: Make Backlinks and make as much as you can. (Put anchors as a main keyword)

Tip: Comment Backlinks are best for Event Blogging. You can use directory submissions as well.

=> Regular Content Update: Update your content on regular basis.

=> Social Shares on Posts: This is one the things that Google loves. Social shares helps a lot in rankings so share your posts on social media platforms. (Google Plus helps me a lot)
